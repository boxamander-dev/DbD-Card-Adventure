Loadout Card Settings

Keyword cards only allow using perks/add-ons that appear upon searching the designated keyword within the DbD search bar;
Otherwise, the search bar should remain empty.


The value in parenthesis at the end is the cost to play the card. A negative number card will provide restrictions, but allow higher costs.

You can only play a Loadout card if you meet its criteria; You cannot have conflicting cards. Max limit of 9 cards per type.
