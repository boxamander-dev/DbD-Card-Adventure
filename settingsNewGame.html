Loadout Cards (Max amount of 9 cards per category)

Keyword cards only allow using perks/add-ons that appear upon searching the designated keyword within the DbD search bar.
The value is parenthesis is the cost to play the card. A negative number card will provide restrictions, but allow higher costs.
You can only play a Loadout card if you meet its criteria; You cannot have conflicting cards.
